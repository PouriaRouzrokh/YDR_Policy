Gonadal Shielding Recommendations for Diagnostic Xray or CT

This page contains guidelines that address gonadal shielding for patients undergoing diagnostic Xray or CT examinations It is part of the Radiology  Biomedical Imaging patient care policies at Yale The recommendations are intended to ensure that patients are informed about and receive appropriate shielding when undergoing imaging procedures
